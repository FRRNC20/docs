## New features

**AI Website Studio.** You can now open the Studio, describe edits to your marketing pages in plain English, preview a live draft, and publish when ready. The Studio uses a bounded, tenant-scoped toolset, so every change is validated before it saves. Publishing stays an explicit operator action. See the [Quickstart](/quickstart) to get started.

**Multi-tenant marketing pages.** Each tenant now has isolated draft and published content. Your public marketing pages render from your own tenant's content at `/sites/<your-tenant>/<path>`, and drafts stay hidden from the public site until you publish.

**Complete 13-section homepage block library.** The block kit now ships every section you need for a full homepage:

- Header and footer with brand chrome, navigation, and trust badges
- Hero band with a rotating-word headline and feature list
- Product cards, category tiles, and the treatment showcase carousel
- Weight-loss plan feature band and green CTA banner
- BMI calculator with a projected-loss slider and compliance disclaimer
- Before/after results marquee
- Blog carousel, FAQ accordion, and benefits marquee

You can edit every section from the Studio.

## Updates

**Studio now runs on Fireworks GLM-5.2.** The Studio chat model is now GLM-5.2 via Fireworks, which gives you faster tool-calling and better instruction following. No action needed — your existing Studio workflows continue to work.

**Production imagery in the block kit.** Product, category, plan, CTA, and blog blocks now ship with production imagery instead of placeholders.

## Bug fixes

- Fixed hero and blog sections silently disappearing when optional image fields were left empty. Optional images now render correctly whether you set them or leave them blank.
- Fixed a rendering issue that caused blocks with unset optional fields to be skipped on the public site.
