<Update label="July 8, 2026">

## 🚀 New features

**One inbox per patient in the care messenger**

The care messenger inbox is now organized around the patient, not the thread. Every channel a patient writes on — in-app, SMS, email — rolls up under one grouped row per person. The conversation pane shows a thread strip with each of that patient's threads and their per-thread unread state. The patient's name in the thread header and details panel now links directly to their profile, so you can jump from a message to the chart in one click.

Each patient also has exactly **one canonical in-app conversation** that is reused forever, labeled **"In app"** across the inbox, thread strip, and details panel. Composing a new in-app message no longer creates a new thread — it appends to the patient's existing "Your care team" conversation.

**SMS ↔ in-app mirror**

When a patient opts in, the SMS and in-app planes merge into a single conversation:

- **Outbound:** A care-team reply sent from the in-app thread also fans out as a real SMS to the patient's phone, shadowed on the same canonical conversation. The in-app message keeps its full length; the SMS copy is truncated to a single segment at the dispatch boundary.
- **Inbound:** When a patient texts back, the SMS is routed into the same in-app "care" thread instead of a separate SMS-only thread.

The result: patients and staff see one unified conversation regardless of channel. The mirror requires the patient to have a phone on file, not be opted out of care SMS, and Twilio to be configured for the tenant.

## ✨ Updates

**Care notifications are now throttled**

Contentless "you have a new message" notifications to patients are now capped at **one per hour per patient**. An unread catch-up sweep still pings a patient who received messages during the quiet window once the hour is out. When the SMS mirror is on and a message is delivered as SMS, that SMS *is* the notification — no duplicate ping is sent. If the SMS fails, the throttled contentless ping is used as a fallback.

- Notifications now also fan out over SMS, not just in-app.
- The catch-up sweep runs on a bounded 5-minute cadence and coalesces pending notifications per patient.

</Update>

<Update label="July 4, 2026">

## 🚀 New features

**AI Website Studio**

Build and edit your site through a chat-driven experience. Ask for changes in plain language, preview them instantly, and publish when ready — no manual layout work required. Powered by Fireworks GLM-5.2.

**Multi-tenant workspaces**

Each brand now runs in its own isolated tenant with scoped drafts, publishing, and public rendering. Content and configuration stay separated across organizations by default.

**Block-based homepage builder**

Compose landing pages from a full library of ready-made blocks:

- Hero, Header, and Footer for site chrome
- FAQ and Blog Posts sections
- Benefits Marquee and Treatment Cards
- Product and Category cards
- Weight Loss Plan and CTA Banner
- BMI Calculator and Results carousel

Mix and match blocks to ship a complete 13-section homepage.

## ✨ Updates

- **Tenant host resolution** — Configured primary app hostnames now resolve to the correct default organization automatically.
- **Draft & publish workflow** — Tenant-scoped draft and publish controls let teams stage changes before they go live.
- **Public site rendering** — Published pages render on tenant-scoped public routes.

## 🐛 Bug fixes

- Content with empty fields now renders correctly instead of breaking the page.
- Tightened tenant isolation so records only appear in the workspace they belong to.
- Resolved a configuration loading issue that could prevent the app from starting in certain environments.

</Update>
